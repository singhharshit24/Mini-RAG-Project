AI Impact Summit India 2026
Comprehensive Overview Document

The AI Impact Summit India 2026 is a major international conference focused on advancements in artificial intelligence and its societal, economic, and governance impact. The summit is scheduled to take place from 16–21 February 2026 at Bharat Mandapam, Pragati Maidan, New Delhi, India.

Venue and Organisers
The event will be hosted at Bharat Mandapam, one of India’s largest international convention centres. The summit is organised under the IndiaAI Mission and supported by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India. The objective is to promote responsible, inclusive, and innovation-driven AI development.

Dates and Format
Dates: 16–21 February 2026
Location: Bharat Mandapam, Pragati Maidan, New Delhi

The summit will include keynote speeches, thematic panel discussions, technical workshops, startup exhibitions, investor sessions, and bilateral meetings between policymakers, researchers, and industry leaders.

Participation and Attendance
The summit is expected to host over 20 heads of state, more than 60 ministers, and approximately 300,000 attendees across six days. Participants include global AI researchers, startup founders, enterprise leaders, policymakers, and academic institutions.

Technology companies, research labs, and AI startups from India and abroad are expected to showcase innovations across sectors including healthcare, agriculture, fintech, cybersecurity, climate modeling, robotics, and generative AI.

Key Themes and Objectives
The summit will focus on the following themes:

Responsible and ethical AI development

AI governance and regulatory frameworks

AI for social good (healthcare, agriculture, education, public services)

Global AI collaboration, especially involving Global South nations

AI safety, transparency, and accountability

Infrastructure development for AI research and compute

The summit aims to position India as a global hub for inclusive AI innovation and digital public infrastructure.

Investment and Economic Announcements
Significant investment commitments are expected in AI infrastructure, including data centres, cloud infrastructure expansion, AI research labs, semiconductor manufacturing initiatives, and startup funding programs.

Announcements are anticipated regarding public-private partnerships, AI research grants, and international collaborations in AI safety and policy development.

Startup and Innovation Showcase
The summit will feature more than 300 exhibitions and demonstrations from startups, universities, research organisations, and multinational companies. Live demonstrations will highlight advancements in generative AI, robotics, computer vision, language models, and AI-enabled public service platforms.

High-Level Dialogues
The summit will host closed-door roundtables and global policy discussions focusing on:

AI regulation and cross-border cooperation

Ethical AI deployment

Bridging AI access gaps between developed and developing economies

AI talent development and education

Significance
The AI Impact Summit India 2026 represents a strategic milestone in India’s technology diplomacy efforts. It reinforces India's ambition to become a leader in AI innovation, infrastructure development, and responsible technology governance.

The event serves as a platform to align global stakeholders toward building AI systems that are safe, equitable, and beneficial to humanity.
